An Aristocrat: Atama Paparangi


1933


Atama Paparangi, A Chieftain of the Rarawa Tribe.

At Lord Bledisloe's prompting, Goldie sent three paintings to London for the 1934 exhibition at the Royal Academy of Arts. This was one of the three, which were 'very well hung and much commented on'.

Painted in his mid-sixties, it's part of a distinctive body of work marked by a warmer palette and considerably thinner application of paint.
