Welcome


Welcome to the Great Art project. The goal of this project is to share the incridible art collection of Hubert Humperdinck. Over the years, Hubert has gathered a large collection of very high quality art. This project is the result of his desire to share his collection with the world.

This particular site is dedicated to the work of Charles Frederick Goldie, one of New Zealand's most highly esteemed artists. Famous for using deep oil paints to create incredibly lifelink images, his works can sell in excess of NZD$1,000,000. Those pieces that Hubert has managed to aquire are displayed here on this website.
